Website Critiques - Chandler Ash

Questions:

  1. What was your first impression of this site?
  2. How does this site organize the flow of information? (Do they have links on the left and text on the right? Some other scheme? No scheme?)
  3. Does the site structure make sense or does it confuse you? Why?
  4. Does the site graphics/layout/multimedia enhance the site or detract from it? Why?
  5. How long did it take for the home page to load? Was the load time reasonable?
  6. Was the text color and size readable? If a background color or image was used, did it enhance or detract from the site?
  7. Did the site have a navigation bar or other navigation tool? Was the navigation well done?
  8. Did the site have a color scheme or look and feel that was repeated on all the pages?
  9. How does the site respond to smaller window sizes (responsive design)?
  10. What does this site offer that was unusual or very well done?


Trader Joes Critique Answers (traderjoes.com):

https://www.traderjoes.com/home

  1. My first impression of this site is that it has clean composition. It is easy to look at and understand.
  2. This site organizes the information in a concise and great way its not too cluttered.
  3. The site structure makes a lot of sense to me. The hamburger button on the top left is a great touch
  4. The coloring and graphics of the website don't feel muddy or cluttered. The design and coloring is pleasing to the eyes.
  5. The home page loaded pretty quick. I have a quick wired connection to the internet. Trader Joe's servers seem to be good too.
  6. The text has a good contrast between coloring of the background and the text. It's easy to read the text.
  7. The hamburger menu on the website provides links to products and recipes which helps the customer and overall goal of Trader Joe's
  8. Some elements of the colors on the home page carry over such as the font and embedded images.
  9. The essential elements of the website respond well to window resizing.
  10. When the page is fullscreen there is a dropdown menu just below the hamburger button that allows you to navigate to the same places as the hamburger menu.
